# Code of Conduct

This project expects practical, respectful collaboration.

## Standards

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Keep discussions focused on the code, documentation and user impact.
- Assume mistakes are fixable, but do not ignore repeated harmful behavior.
- Do not harass, threaten, insult or publish private information.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide or remove comments and contributions that violate these standards. Repeated or severe violations may lead to a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.

If you need to report a problem, contact a maintainer privately with the relevant context, links and timestamps.
